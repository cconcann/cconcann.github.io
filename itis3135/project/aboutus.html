About MG Photography!

I graduated from Methodist University located in Fayetteville, NC in 2017. I have been modeling on and off for the past 12 years, which sparked my passion for photography. Over the past 2 years my desire to capture the perfect moment has fueled my passion to expand my photography business. In the last 2 years I have worked hard to learn the various Lense techniques to be able to create an immersive experience for my clients. I enjoy shooting anything from nature, to animals, to babies, and people. I am ready to personalize your modeling experience, book your captivating shoot with me today!
