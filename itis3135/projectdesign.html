Project Design!

Step 1 Project overview.

  • The webpage is to support and give a professional website for a friends photography hobby.
  • The intended users are those that need a photographer for their special occasions
  • The content will be a basic service based website to showcase her work, and give people information about her business.

Client Information.

  • The clients name is Morgan Gilbert.
  • She is associated with MG photography.
  • Email address is morgangphotos@gmail.com
  • phone number- private.

Home page info.

  • The purpose is to be a nice landing page to keep people involved and interested.
  • The audience is any possible customers or models wanting photos done.
  • No data is asked for on this page.
  • This page will have validation buttons at the bottom.
  • No real actions will occur here, just a nice looking homepage to navigate from.

About us info.

  • The purpose is to let the user learn about the company and the people.
  • The audience is those who are still interested and want to learn about the photographer.
  • No data is asked for or input.
  • This page will have validation buttons at the bottom.
  • No real actions will occur here.

photos info.

  • The purpose is to showcase a nice interactive showcase of her photos.
  • The audience is those who want to verify her capabilities as a photographer.
  • No data is asked for on this page.
  • This page will have validation buttons at the bottom. also the slideshow will have buttons to navigate as well.
  • The interaction here will be the slideshow navigation and auto navigation

Pricing page info.

  • The purpose is to provide pricing and package information for potential clients.
  • The audience is potential clients.
  • No data is asked for on this page.
  • This page will have validation buttons at the bottom.
  • No real actions will occur here, just a nice looking page for people to see general pricing.

contact page info.

  • The purpose is to provide users a way of contacting the owner, by form or listed information.
  • The audience is any possible customers or models wanting photos done.
  • Users have the option to fill out a form to have the photographer reach out to them. no validations required.
  • This page will have validation buttons at the bottom. Also it will have a form on the page to allow potential clients to input their data and comments.
  • The page will take input from the users and have a submit button that will notionally get sent to the photographer.

Dynamic functionality.

The only real DF on this website is the contact us and photos page. the photos has a nice interactive slideshow. the contact us will have some forms that will allow potential clients the ability to fill out a form that will have all the normal information that a photographer would need to address specific situations.
